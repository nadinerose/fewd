Questions for YOU to answer:

There are lots of questions that come up in class that aren't necessarily worth answering in the moment or are a great opportunity for you to learn something new and explain it to the class. Pick a question, find the answer, and then put together an explanation that the whole class can understand.

  • Why does HTML 5 doctype not include the version?
  • What does the "rel" attribute on <link> do?
  • What does "find in buffer" mean?
  • What is SGML (Standard Generalized Markup Language)
  • What else can a <link> tag link to?
  • What are the top three techniques for making a website more accessible?
  • Do self-closing tags need a trailing /?
